Refresh

Stay in touch with the people you care about through phone calls.

Find Out More

We've got what you need!

At Refresh, we believe in technology that helps you maintain meaningful relationships in a better way. To help you, we've built an iOS app that makes it incredibly easy for you to stay in touch with your friends through phone calls.

Whether you are a recent college graduate or a busy venture capitalist, whether you are waiting for a bus or looking to catch up over the weekend, all you have to do is open Refresh. You decide who you want to stay in touch with, and we'll do all the heavy lifting.

Get Started!

Main Features

Import Contacts

Import contacts you want to stay in touch with directly from the Address Book of your iPhone.

Know Who to Call

Based on your preferences and call history, we show you a sorted contact list so you know who to call.

Save Call Information

After each call, you can save a quick note and any special dates to call them in the future.

Show Availability

If there's no one you want to call immediately, set a timer for when you're available and your friends will call you!

Your information will always be safe.

Using our secure phone verification procedure, only your device has access to your Refresh account. Sensitive contact information is only stored locally on your phone.

And don't worry, only your friends will be able to see your availability.
